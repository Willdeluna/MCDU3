# LNAV And VNAV Roadmap

LNAV/VNAV are trainer-grade deterministic models.

LNAV direction:

- Active waypoint and next waypoint.
- Direct-to state.
- Discontinuity stop behavior.
- ND active segment highlighting.
- PROG page distance and destination values.

VNAV direction:

- Constraint parsing.
- Simplified climb/descent feasibility.
- T/C and T/D cues.
- `UNABLE NEXT ALT`, `DRAG REQUIRED`, and `PERF/VNAV UNAVAILABLE` messages.

These models are not operational guidance systems.
